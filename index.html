Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate student (5th year) in Center for Research on Intelligent Perception and Computing ( CRIPAC ), National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition ( NLPR), Institute of Automation Chinese Academy of Sciences ( CASIA ), advised by Prof. Kaiqi Huang. I received my Bachelor degree from the Communication Engineering Department, of JiLin University (JLU), in 2013.

My research interests are in computer vision and machine learning, especially in deep learning based vision applications, such as recognition, detection and human parsing. My recent work mainly focues on person middle attribute recognition and person re-identification.
